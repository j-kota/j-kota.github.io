James Kotary

Welcome to my research homepage.

I am a PhD candidate at Syracuse, where I am interested in integrating constrained optimization and machine learning models to enable new functionality in AI. Lately, my research has focused on learning fast approximations to hard optimization problems, and using constrained optimization as layer in neural networks to learn data-driven optimal decision making. My research advisor is the great Ferdinando Fioretto.
